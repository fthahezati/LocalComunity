WELCOME TO WEBSITE OF LOCAL COMMUNITY OF KAMPUNG PADANG PUTEH LANGKAWI, KEDAH.



⭐A local community is a group of people who live in the same geographical area or a group of people who share certain characteristics such as common religion, social ties, common perspectives, leadership and engage in joint actions or work in the same setting.
⭐Kampung Padang Putih is a village located in Langkawi, in the state of Kedah Darul Aman, which is a state in the north of Malaysia, and which borders Thailand in the northeast, and borders the state of Perak in the southeast, Penang in the southwest and Perlis in the northern part. Kedah is a state known as a developed agricultural state in Malaysia, especially as the largest paddy producer, producing 70% of paddy production in 2017. One of its special traditional foods is Kedah laksa.
